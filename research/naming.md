# App Name Brainstorm
**Created:** 2026-02-22 by Lens (market research agent)

---

## Naming Criteria

Good names in this space are:
- Short (1–2 words, ideally 1)
- Warm, not clinical
- Easy to say and spell
- Not already taken as an active App Store app
- Evoke simplicity, calm, or gentle progress — not urgency or pain

**Avoid:** anything too medical, anything with "Health Pro" energy, anything that sounds like a hospital app.

---

## Top Candidates

### 🥇 Ebb
- **Why:** "The ebb and flow of how I feel each day" — resonates deeply with chronic illness experience
- **Feel:** Minimal, calm, beautiful
- **App Store:** No confirmed active app with this name in Health & Fitness
- **Domain:** ebb.app may be taken; ebb.health or getebb.com likely available
- **Risk:** Very short names can be hard to trademark — check USPTO

### 🥈 Flarely
- **Why:** Invented word — warm, clearly about flares, distinctive
- **Feel:** Friendly, approachable
- **App Store:** No results found in research
- **Domain:** flarely.com — likely available
- **Risk:** Low — invented word, probably clear of existing trademarks

### 🥉 Steadily
- **Why:** Emotional resonance — "I track steadily, I improve steadily"
- **Feel:** Hopeful, calm, motivating
- **App Store:** Check — may exist as a general productivity app
- **Domain:** steadily.com — check availability

### Also Consider
| Name | Notes |
|---|---|
| **ClearLog** | Functional, descriptive, easy to search |
| **Notem** | App-y feel, "note them" connotation, short |
| **Settle** | Calm vibe, but could read as giving up |
| **Softer** | Interesting but might confuse |
| **Kindly** | Warm but reads more like a messaging app |

---

## Names to Avoid
- **Flaredown** — already exists as a web brand (appears abandoned but name is taken)
- **Bearable** — obviously taken
- **Luma** — taken by ADHD app
- Anything with "Pain" in the name — clinical, discourages casual users

---

## Recommendation

**Go with `Ebb`** if it's clear on the App Store. It's the most distinctive, memorable, and emotionally resonant name in the list. If `Ebb` is taken, `Flarely` is a strong fallback that's clearly original.

**Full App Store title:** `Ebb: Symptom & Flare Tracker`
This puts the brand first AND includes the two most searchable keywords.
