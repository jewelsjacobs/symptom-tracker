# Symptom Tracker — Color System
**Theme: Warm Topographic**
*Completely unclaimed in the App Store — all 4 major competitors use cool blues, teals, or purple.*

---

## Core Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
|------|------|-----|-------|
| Primary | Coral | `#E8725A` | Buttons, key accents, brand moments |
| Primary Dark | Terracotta | `#C2553F` | Pressed states, dark emphasis |
| Primary Light | Peach | `#FADED4` | Light fills, subtle backgrounds, tags |
| Secondary | Amber | `#F5A962` | Secondary charts, warm data viz |
| Complementary | Sage | `#7EB8A4` | "Good" states, calm/positive data |
| Background | Cream | `#FDF8F5` | App background (NOT pure white) |
| Surface | White | `#FFFFFF` | Cards float on warm cream |
| Text Primary | Warm Black | `#2D2926` | Headlines, body text |
| Text Secondary | Warm Gray | `#7A706B` | Subtitles, metadata, labels |

## Severity Scale (symptom intensity)

| Level | Color | Hex | Meaning |
|-------|-------|-----|---------|
| Low | Sage | `#E6F4F0` / `#3A8A74` | Calm, manageable |
| Moderate | Amber | `#FEF3E6` / `#C97C20` | Noticeable |
| High | Coral | `#FADED4` / `#C2553F` | Significant |
| Severe | Deep Coral | `#E8725A` white text | Intense |

*Natural warm-to-hot metaphor — no harsh red/green binary.*

---

## Typography (recommended)

- **Headlines:** Instrument Sans or Satoshi — humanist, friendly, NOT SF Pro
- **Body:** Clean geometric sans-serif (Geist, Inter) at generous line-height
- **Data/numbers:** Tabular figures (monospace-feel) for chart values
- **Section labels:** Generous letter-spacing (1–1.5px), uppercase, small

---

## Card / Component Style

- **Border radius:** 16px for cards, 14px for buttons, 20px for pill tags
- **Shadows:** Warm-tinted (rgba(194,85,63,0.08)) — NOT cool gray shadows
- **Elevation:** Layered "topographic" stacking — cards at different altitudes
- **No hard borders** — define boundaries with color shifts + elevation only

---

## Data Visualization — "Terrain Maps of Health"

- **Area charts with layered fills** (geological strata) — echoes topographic icon
- **Smoothed/curved lines** — health as landscape, not stock ticker
- **Stacked symptom layers** — each symptom is a terrain layer
- Avoid: sharp bar charts, circular progress rings (everyone else's default)
